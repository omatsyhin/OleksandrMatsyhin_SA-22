ABOUT US

Crispy Bakery is a place where taste, quality, and tradition come together in every product. We began our journey with the goal of creating a "space for happiness" in the heart of Lviv. Our experienced bakers constantly refine recipes and implement new technologies to bring you fresh pastries that delight not only the palate but also the soul.

At CRISPY, we bake our products in traditional ovens, replenishing our shelves every morning. Our assortment includes everything: fragrant bread, delicate sweet pastries, and crispy cookies. Visit us to grab some treats "to-go" or enjoy them in the cozy atmosphere of our bakery with a cup of aromatic coffee or tea.
